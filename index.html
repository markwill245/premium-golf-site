The Club

One hundred and thirty-two years of unhurried golf.

Set on the undulating parkland above Dudley, our course rewards thought before power. Eighteen distinctive holes weave through mature oak and lime, with sweeping views over the Black Country that stop new visitors in their tracks.

The clubhouse, the starters, the members — all carry a welcome that speaks of a club comfortable in its traditions, and confident in its future.
